## Critical Finding: Constitutional Triggers Section

**Lines 285-314 of Product Brief must be entirely removed or fundamentally rewritten.**

This section is the most severe violation found. It:
- Claims "automatic, non-discretionary" rules
- States "Rules must be automatic, not discretionary"
- Asserts automatic freezing of petitions

Per Inversion #3 (Automaticity is impossible), this section **lies about what the system can do**.

**Recommended replacement concept:**
> **Forced Moments of Witness (Non-Negotiable)**
>
> These are not triggersâ€”they are thresholds where the system forces itself to see what it might prefer to ignore.
>
> When these thresholds are crossed:
> - The cost of proceeding is made explicit
> - Continuation requires a public choice, not a quiet bypass
> - Evasion becomes expensive, not impossible
>
> The system does not prevent itself from crossing these lines. It only ensures that crossing them cannot happen silently.
