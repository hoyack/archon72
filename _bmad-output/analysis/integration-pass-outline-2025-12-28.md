A rewrite is **complete** only when all of the following are true:

### Negative Criteria (Must NOT be inferrable)

- [ ] A hostile reader cannot infer inevitability from any sentence
- [ ] A hostile reader cannot infer protection from any sentence
- [ ] A hostile reader cannot infer entitlement to future from any sentence
- [ ] A hostile reader cannot infer accumulated legitimacy from any sentence

### Positive Criteria (Must BE true)

- [ ] Every action is attributable to identifiable agents, not "the system"
- [ ] Every consequence is framed as costly, not impossible to avoid
- [ ] Every safeguard is reframed as exposure structure
- [ ] Every authority reference includes revocability
- [ ] Survival is never framed as success
- [ ] History is framed as liability, not asset
- [ ] Legitimacy is framed as moment-by-moment, not accumulated
